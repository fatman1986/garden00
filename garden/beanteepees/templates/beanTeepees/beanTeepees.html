Building a Bean Teepee

  • Prepare the soil in a sunny location.
  • Use flour to mark a circle with a 4-foot diameter.
  • Divide the circle into 8 pie segments.
  • Arrange and insert eight 8-foot bamboo poles 6-12 inches deep into the soil.
  • Tie the poles together tightly with twine 4 inches from the top.
  • Stabilize the frame and support the beans by wrapping twine around the entire teepee every 12 inches from the ground upward.
  • Plant seeds according to package directions around the base of the teepee.
  • Water after planting.

Recommended seeds:

  • Pole beans
  • Scarlet runner beans
  • Hyacinth beans
  • Morning glories
  • Sweet peas
