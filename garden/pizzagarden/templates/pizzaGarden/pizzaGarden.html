Growing a Pizza Garden

  • Vegetables need a sunny growing spot.
  • After preparing the soil, outline a circular bed with flour.
  • Divide the circle into pizza wedges.
  • Plant ingredients as seeds or bedding plants spacing according to plant needs.
  • Water thoroughly.

Suggested ingredients:

  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Garlic chives
  • Italian parsley
  • Marjoram
  • Onions
  • Peppers
  • Tomatoes
